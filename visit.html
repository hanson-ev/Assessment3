Why Visit Stonehaven

Whether you’re after a picturesque coastal or woodland trail that is suitable for the whole family, are a history buff searching for architecture and artifacts lost to time, or simply looking to grab an ice cream for a walk down the beach, there is plenty to do in Stonehaven.
Foodies will be spoiled for choice by the number of family-owned cafes, restaurants and more. For example, did you know that Stonehaven’s The Carron Fish Bar is the birthplace of a true Scottish classic – the deep fried Mars bar?

The weather in Stonehaven is at its best in the UK’s summer months – in particular from July until September, where the weather is at its warmest.
However, winter at Stonehaven is far from uneventful! The Hogmanay Fireball Ceremony brings in visitors from around the world every year, and is truly a sight to behold!

Getting to Stonehaven
By bus:
From Aberdeen, Stagecoach buses 7, X7, and 7B.
By train:
Regular hourly trains arrive to Stonehaven from both Dundee and Aberdeen.
By car:
Head south from Aberdeen via the A90 main road.
